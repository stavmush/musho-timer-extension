Privacy Policy

The Timer Bar Extension is committed to user privacy and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user activity. The extension operates entirely within the user's browser and does not require any external data connections or permissions beyond what is necessary to display and control the timer.

Data Collection and Use

The Timer Bar Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. It operates entirely within the user's browser, ensuring full privacy.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions solely to perform its functions:

  • activeTab: To inject the timer bar into the active webpage.
  • scripting: To inject the timer's JavaScript and CSS into the webpage.
